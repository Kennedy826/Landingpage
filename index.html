Services

Collection Scheduling

Users can schedule pickups or drop-off appointments for their e-waste items at designated collection points or recycling centers. The app may provide a calendar or scheduling system to choose convenient dates and times.

Eco-Friendly Recycling Information

The app can provide information on the environmental benefits of recycling e-waste and how the recycling process works, raising awareness among users.

Location Finder

The app can help users locate the nearest e-waste recycling centers, collection points, or participating retailers where they can drop off their e-waste items.

About E-waste Smart App

As the world continues to battle effects of environmental degradation as a result of poor waste disposal, there is need for immediate remedies that would ensure environment is protected from any form of damage be it on the energy consumption, industrial emittance and outdated agricultural practices. The solutions for these problems can be realized through efficiemnt and economically viable applications that would ensure anyone who has access to smartphone can do waste disporsal efficiently. E-Waste Smart App offers a range of services and features to facilitate the responsible disposal, recycling, and management of waste.
